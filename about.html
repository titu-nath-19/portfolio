About Me

As a recent Mechanical Engineering graduate from KUET with a strong academic record (CGPA: 3.65/4.00), I have a proven history of success in national and international technical competitions and leadership experience that showcases my innovative thinking and dedication to excellence. My passion lies in biomechanics, bioengineering, cardiovascular fluid mechanics, hemodynamics, advanced fluid dynamics, multiphase flow, aerodynamics, heat-transfer problems, and composite materials.

While maintaining academic excellence in research, I've actively led formula student racing teams and innovative engineering projects that have sharpened my technical expertise and problem-solving capabilities. Complementing my professional development, my dedication to organizing life-saving blood donation networks during emergencies has instilled a profound sense of social responsibility. This unique combination of engineering acumen and humanitarian commitment drives my ambition to develop solutions that address complex technical challenges while creating meaningful impact for society.
